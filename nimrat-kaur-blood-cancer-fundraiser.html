Nimrat's Medical Battle

"My name is Nimrat Kaur, and I am just 2 years old. I am fighting blood cancer and need your urgent support. I am a Gursikh child, doing my best to stay strong during this difficult time. My family needs ₹7,0,000 for my treatment and life-saving care, which is more than we can afford. Please help me get a chance to live, grow, and experience the world. Even a small contribution can help save my life."

Little Nimrat, at just 24 months old, has been diagnosed with aggressive blood cancer (Leukemia). Instead of playing with toys and discovering the world, her days are filled with sterile hospital rooms, heavy chemotherapy sessions, and painful treatments.

Her father, a humble Gursikh Sikh devotee from Punjab, earns a meager livelihood and has stretched every resource. The total estimated cost of her chemotherapy cycle, pediatric oncology care, medicines, and life-saving hospital stay is **₹7,00,000 (INR 7 Lakhs)**. This financial weight is entirely out of outreach for the family.
